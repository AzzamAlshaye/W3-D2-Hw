my story

Title description, April 7, 2014

My name is Azzam Alshaye. I'm originally from Al Mithnab, a beautiful town located in the Al-Qassim Region of Saudi Arabia. Although my roots are in Al Mithnab, I was raised in Riyadh, the capital city where I have spent my entire life. Growing up in Riyadh gave me many opportunities to experience city life, education, and modern culture, while still maintaining a strong connection to my hometown. During vacations and holidays, I always make it a point to return to Al Mithnab, where I reconnect with my family, traditions, and the quieter lifestyle. Both Riyadh and Al Mithnab hold a special place in my heart, and they have shaped who I am today.
